Hockey Club Manager

Free-to-play, play-to-earn, NFT-based fantasy hockey managers game.

About

Hockey Club Manager is a sports manager simulator, where you can rule your own hockey team. Find your players, build the team, and win games!

Our mission is to create a full-filled experience by adding a wide range of game mechanics.
